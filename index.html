🟦 What is Arduino?

Arduino is a tiny computer brain that you can program to do ANYTHING!

👆 Drag to rotate the board!

🔵 Key Parts of Arduino UNO
  • Microcontroller (ATmega328P) — The brain! Runs your code.
  • USB Port — Connects to your computer to upload programs.
  • Reset Button — Press to restart your program.
  • Digital Pins (0–13) — Like light switches: ON or OFF.
  • Analog Pins (A0–A5) — Read values like temperature and light.
  • LEDs — Tiny lights that tell you it's working.
  • Capacitors — Store a tiny bit of electricity, like a mini battery.

💻 Your First Arduino Code!
void setup() {
  // This runs ONCE when you start
  pinMode(13, OUTPUT); // LED pin
}

void loop() {
  // This runs FOREVER
  digitalWrite(13, HIGH); // 💡 ON
  delay(1000);  // wait 1 second
  digitalWrite(13, LOW);  // 💡 OFF
  delay(1000);  // wait 1 second
}
⬆️ This code makes an LED blink — ON and OFF, forever! ✨

📡 What is ESP32?

ESP32 is a super smart chip — it's like Arduino, but it can connect to WiFi and Bluetooth too!

🌟 What's special about ESP32?
  • WiFi Built-in — Can connect to internet like your phone!
  • Bluetooth — Talks to other devices wirelessly!
  • Dual Core CPU — TWO brains in one chip! Super fast!
  • 38 GPIO Pins — Lots of connections for sensors!
  • Touch Pins — Can detect your finger touch!

🔮 Cool Sensors!

Sensors are like the eyes, ears, and nose of a robot — they feel the world! 👀👂👃
Click on any sensor to learn more!

📡 Ultrasonic Sensor
Measures distance using sound waves!
🎵 It sends out a sound (too high for us to hear!) and listens for the echo — just like a bat! The longer the echo takes, the farther away the object is!
Used in: Robot cars to avoid walls!
🌡️ Temperature Sensor
Feels how hot or cold it is!
🌞❄️ The DHT11 sensor measures both temperature AND how much water is in the air (humidity)! It works just like a thermometer at the doctor's office.
Used in: Smart home AC systems!
☀️ Light Sensor (LDR)
Detects how bright or dark it is!
💡 LDR means Light Dependent Resistor. When it's bright, electricity flows easily. When it's dark, it blocks electricity — like a little light meter!
Used in: Street lights that turn ON at night!
👁️ PIR Motion Sensor
Detects when someone moves!
🏃 PIR stands for Passive InfraRed. Our bodies give off heat, and this sensor can feel that heat from far away — it knows when you walk by!
Used in: Automatic bathroom lights!
💧 Moisture Sensor
Checks if soil is wet or dry!
🌱 Put the two metal prongs into soil — if it's wet, electricity flows between them. If it's dry, no flow! Perfect for knowing when your plant needs water!
Used in: Smart plant watering robots!
🔔 Buzzer
Makes beeping sounds when told to!
🎵 A buzzer has a tiny vibrating disk inside. When electricity flows through it, it vibrates super fast and makes a BEEP sound! You control how fast it beeps.
Used in: Alarm clocks & doorbells!
